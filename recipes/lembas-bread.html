ELVEN LEMBAS BREAD

A Delicious and filling bread, made from the Elves in The Lord of the Rings.

Legolas: Lembas. Elvish waybread. One small bite is enough to fill the stomach of a grown man.
Merry: How many did you eat?
Pippin: Four.

The perfect addition to Breakfast, Second Breakfast, Elevenses, Luncheon, Afternoon Tea, Dinner, or Supper. Or All of the above.

Ingredients

  • 3 Gondorian Eggs
  • 1 cup of Hobbiton Honey
  • 3 Khazad-DÃ»m Kumquats, whole
  • 3 oz. chopped Rohan-Roasted Almonds
  • 1/4 cup melted Bree Butter
  • 2 1/4 cup Flour
  • 1/2 tsp. Salt

Steps

  1. Put the eggs, butter, honey, kumquats, and nuts in a food processor or blender
  2. Blend on high for 2-4 minutes
  3. Add 1 cup of the flour and blend for a minute or two
  4. Put mixture into a bowl and add the remaining flour and the salt, Whisk or stir until well blended
  5. Bake a small amount of dough at a time on an iron about 15 seconds or until lightly brown, for a flat bread like texture
    They can also be baked at 350 degrees for about 10-15 minutes
  6. Wrap in a leaf and tie with a string!
