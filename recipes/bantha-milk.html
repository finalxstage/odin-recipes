BANTHA MILK

Sith Kyber Crystal's are Red, Bantha Milk is Blue, but don't get it confused with R2-D2

Luke Skywalker's breakfast, lunch, and dinner "Drink-of-Choice."

Except when he drinks Thala-Siren-Milk. We don't talk about that though, or The Last Jedi in general. Don't Click either of those.

Pair it nicely with Roasted Porg.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk, straight from a Bantha's teet
  • 1 tsp of Kessel Mine Sugar
  • 3 cubes of Hoth Ice
  • 1 expensive trip to Tattoine, or Galaxy's Edge in Disney
  • An arm or a leg, depending on how much Disney costs (can also be used to stir drink)

Steps

  1. Fill your favorite cup with Hoth Ice, unless preferred warm
  2. Proceed to Tatooine Desert, preferably outside of Mos Espa
  3. Sneak past any Tuskan Raider guards, locate female Bantha, and milk directly into your favorite cup
  4. Add 1 tsp of Kessel Mine Sugar. Add additional if preferred sweeter taste.
    Just try not to think about the Kessel Slaves who mined that sugar.
